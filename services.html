FAQ

Common Questions

How long is a typical project timeline?
Small renovations: 4-8 weeks. Custom homes: 8-14 months. Commercial towers: 18-36 months. We give you a firm timeline in the proposal.
Do you handle permits?
Yes — end-to-end. Our permit team handles zoning, environmental, and safety approvals as part of every engagement.
What warranty do you offer?
10-year structural warranty on all new builds, 2 years on renovations, and lifetime service call priority for clients.
Do you build green?
Every project targets LEED Silver minimum. We can pursue Gold or Platinum with the right budget and site.
